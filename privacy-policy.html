Introduction

MyVibe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Photos and Images: We process photos you upload or capture through the app to analyze aesthetic style, body structure, and provide personalized aesthetic recommendations. All processing is done locally on your device using Apple's Vision Framework and Core ML.
  • Body Analysis Data: When you use the body analysis feature, our app uses Apple's Vision Framework to analyze body structure, proportions, and measurements. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.
  • Color Analysis Data: The app analyzes colors from your photos to provide style recommendations. All color analysis is performed locally on your device.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our services.
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected for app functionality and troubleshooting.
  • Subscription Information: If you purchase a premium subscription, payment information is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide aesthetic style analysis and personalized recommendations
  • To analyze body structure and proportions for aesthetic profile analysis (all processing occurs locally on your device)
  • To analyze colors from your photos for style recommendations
  • To organize and manage your wardrobe
  • To improve our app's features and user experience
  • To provide customer support
  • To manage premium subscriptions and features

Data Processing and Storage

All data processing occurs locally on your device. Your photos, body analysis data, color analysis data, and personal information are processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision Framework and Core ML. We do not store your photos, body data, color data, or personal information on our servers. All analysis is performed on-device, ensuring your privacy and data security.

Data Retention: Analysis data may be stored locally on your device to provide a better user experience (such as saving your last analysis result). You can clear this data at any time through the app settings or by deleting the app. Photos are not stored permanently unless you explicitly save them within the app.

Camera and Photo Library Access

MyVibe requires access to your camera and photo library to:

  • Take new photos for analysis
  • Select existing photos from your library for analysis
  • Access photos for wardrobe organization

All photos are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties. You can revoke camera or photo library access at any time through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and app functionality. These services have their own privacy policies. Importantly, your photos, body analysis data, and color analysis data are never shared with any third-party services.

Apple App Store: If you purchase a premium subscription, payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Premium Subscriptions

If you purchase a premium subscription through the App Store:

  • Payment information is processed by Apple, not by us
  • We receive information about your subscription status to enable premium features
  • We do not store or have access to your payment details
  • Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID and App Store settings

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, modify, or delete your personal information stored locally on your device
  • Revoke camera or photo library access at any time through your device settings
  • Clear app data through the app settings or by deleting the app
  • Cancel your premium subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns or questions

Children's Privacy

MyVibe is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Support page.
